4G-PAQS Team Meeting
Montreal 2025

Montreal, Canada — Aug 25 – 29, 2025

Université de Montréal

Rationale


The first part of this meeting will be open to the public and will present the core science cases of the 4G-PAQS survey and current status of the 4MOST project. The following closed sessions (only for survey members) will be dedicated to discussions regarding the survey specific data analysis and our deliverable data products.


Preliminary Programme


  • Day 1: Registration and Introduction to the project ending with an opening reception (public)
  • Day 2: Core science presentations (public)
  • Day 3: Science presentations and discussions (closed)
  • Day 4: Social activity (closed) and public outreach talk (public)
  • Day 5: Deliverable data products (closed)
